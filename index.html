  1. Fall, Junior Year

    Kickoff & Discovery

    Values, goals, and the long list. What does your student want? What matters? What are we building toward? This is the foundation everything else sits on.

  2. Winter/Spring, Junior Year

    Research & Outreach

    Campus research, professor outreach, narrowing the school list. Your student develops real criteria for fit and starts making meaningful connections which help narrow and focus the process.

  3. Summer Before Senior Year

    Strategy & Setup

    Final list locked. Application strategy set. Common App prep begins. By September, we're ready to execute.

  4. September–November, Senior Year

    Application Crunch

    Weekly check-ins, deadline tracking, submission. This is where having a plan pays off. No scrambling or last-minute chaos. We execute together.

  5. Senior Spring

    Decisions & Finish Strong

    As acceptances and offers arrive, decisions are made, but the work of graduating isn't done. I help your student keep their eyes on the prize through the end.

What's Included

A shared plan, tracked in real time, from junior year through graduation.

School List Development

Start with 20+ options. Narrow systematically to 8–10 schools that fit academically, culturally, and personally. A list your student actually wants to work through. I help your student with research and filtering this list.

Professor Outreach Coaching

As the list is being reviewed, your student connects with faculty at target schools. This helps them learn about the school and program, and make a meaningful impression on faculty. I coach them through it.

Timeline & Deadline Management

A shared project plan from fall of junior year through submission. You'll always know where things stand, and so will your student.

Structured Check-ins

Lighter touchpoints early, weekly sessions during application crunch (September through November of senior year). Consistent, accountable, predictable.

Parent Communication

Regular updates so you know where things stand, without becoming the one responsible for keeping them on track.

Senior Year Check-in

A dedicated check-in on your student's senior year commitments: grades, activities, anything that could affect admissions. I help them stay focused and on track amid strong bouts of senioritis.
