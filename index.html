What is Clear Path?

Most families experience the college application process as chaotic — a blur of deadlines, competing priorities, and anxiety. Clear Path brings professional project management discipline to the process. You get a real plan, a clear timeline, and someone accountable for keeping it moving.

Professor Outreach Coaching

Timeline & Deadline Management
A shared project plan from fall junior year through submission. Every milestone, mapped.
