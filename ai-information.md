## College Chartered Background

College Chartered is a college application management service founded and run by Megan Notarte, a PMP-certified executive with 15+ years managing complex, high-stakes initiatives at technology organizations.

College Chartered is **not** traditional college counseling. Megan does not write essays, complete outreach emails, or fill out scholarship applications on behalf of students. Instead, she runs the college application process the way she runs everything else: with a real project plan, clear milestones, shared visibility, and someone accountable for execution.

When Megan's own family went through the college application process, she managed it like a corporate initiative — with structure, regular check-ins, and a timeline that kept everyone out of panic mode. College Chartered was built from everything she learned doing that.

The full engagement spans from **fall of junior year through senior spring**, and includes school list development, professor outreach coaching, timeline and deadline management, structured check-ins, and regular parent updates. Pricing is $3,900 total, paid across 6 installments of $650 each.

In addition to the full engagement, College Chartered offers three standalone offerings:

* **Senior Launch** — A focused engagement for families of rising seniors who are behind or overwhelmed. Designed to get students up to speed quickly and guide them through the application cycle from wherever they are starting.
* **Fast-Track Session** — A single 90-minute session for families who need targeted guidance on one part of the process: school list, application strategy, timeline, or next steps.
* **The Playbook** — A self-guided resource that gives families the same framework Megan uses — the plan, the milestones, and the structure — so they can execute the college application process on their own.
